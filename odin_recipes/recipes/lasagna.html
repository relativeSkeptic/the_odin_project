Lasagna

To make lasagna, start by cooking lasagna noodles in salted boiling water until just tender, then drain and set them aside. In a pan, sauté garlic and onion in olive oil, add ground beef or sausage, and cook until browned before stirring in tomato sauce and letting it simmer with herbs like basil and oregano.

In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg, grated Parmesan, salt, and pepper. To assemble, spread a thin layer of sauce in a baking dish, add a layer of noodles, then spread the ricotta mixture, sprinkle mozzarella cheese, and spoon over more sauce; repeat these layers until the dish is full, finishing with sauce and mozzarella on top.

Cover with foil and bake at about 375°F (190°C) for around 25 minutes, then uncover and bake another 10–15 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly; let it rest briefly before slicing and serving.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Boil salted water.
  2. Add pasta.
  3. Cook until tender.
  4. Drain the water.
  5. Add sauce and serve.
