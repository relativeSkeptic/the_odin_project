Hamburger

To make a hamburger, start by forming ground beef into patties and seasoning them with salt and pepper. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat and cook the patties for a few minutes on each side until browned and cooked to your preferred doneness, adding a slice of cheese near the end if desired.

Lightly toast hamburger bun halves, then place the cooked patty on the bottom bun and add toppings like lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, or mustard. Finish by placing the top bun on and serve while hot.

Ingredients

  • Burger Patties
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Form seasoned beef into patties.
  2. Heat a pan or grill.
  3. Cook the patties.
  4. Toast the buns.
  5. Assemble with toppings and serve.
